Frequently Asked Questions

How many walkers/sitters will be assigned to me?

You will have two dedicated walkers/sitters assigned to you. In addition to your dedicated walkers/sitters, we assign a third emergency back up to ensure we always have you covered.

Who will have access to my house keys and property?

We ask each each client to provide three keys. Two keys are assigned to your dedicated walkers/sitters, and the third is stored in our office. All keys are marked without personal information, and stored securely at all times.

Who will enter my house/property?

Only an employee of Paws Around Motown will ever enter your house/property.

How will I know my dog was walked and/or visited while I'm not home?

Paws Around Motown utilizes pet sitting software to keep you updated. Upon completion of our visit, you will receive a text or email update which includes a GPS check-in/check-out, photo of your pet, and visit notes.

Have any other questions?

Feel free to email us, or reach us on Facebook by clicking the Facebook logo below.
