Pricing

Dog Walking

20, 30, 45, or 60 minute on leash exercise in your pet’s neighborhood. Includes waste clean up during walk and, upon request, feeding and home care within the scheduled time frame.

Pricing

  • 20 Minutes - $14
  • 30 Minutes - $16
  • 45 Minutes - $20
  • 60 Minutes - $24
  • Add $5 for visits before 8 am or after 8 pm
  • Add $5 for each additional dog, if more than 2 dogs

Overnight Pet Sitting

Pet sitter spends the night with your pet in your home from approximately 7pm - 7am. Includes private exercise, feeding and water refresh, medication administration, litter box maintenance, mail and newspaper retrieval, light and window treatment rotation, and plant watering.

Pricing

  • $60 per night
  • $70 per night with mid-day walk
  • If you have more than two pets, add $5 for each additional dog and $3 for each additional cat
  • Add $10/hr for early arrival or late departure
  • $15 for each additional visit between the hours of 7am and 7pm

20 Minute Potty Break

Perfect for cats, puppies and senior dogs! This 15-20 minute visit is a good break for your pets and allows them potty time, concentrated play, feeding and watering, litter box clean up, and more during the allotted time.

Pricing

  • $14 per visit
  • Add $5 for visits before 8 am or after 8 pm
  • Add $5 for each additional pet, if more than 2 pets

Vacation Visit Packages

Each 30 minute visit includes private exercise, feeding and water refresh, medication administration, litter box maintenance, mail and newspaper retrieval, light and window treatment rotation, plant watering, and home security checks.

Pricing

  • 2 visits - $32 per day
  • 3 visits - $46 per day
  • 4 visits - $59 per day
  • Add $5 for visits before 8 am or after 8 pm
  • Add $5 for each additional dog, if more than 2 dogs

Home Care

Includes up to 30 minutes of plant watering, mail and newspaper retrieval, light and window treatment rotation, and home security checks.

Pricing

  • $20 per visit
  • No charge if combined within timeframe of another scheduled pet service

Pet Taxi

Transportation of pets and pet care supplies to and from boarding facilities, friends and family, trainers, veterinarians, or other locations.

Pricing

  • $.50 per additional mile (after base miles)
  • $.50 per minute of wait time

Monthly Dog Walking Packages

We offer a discounted package for monthly dog walking services. This package includes twenty walks of either 20, 30, 45, or 60 minute walks. These packages must be paid in full prior to services being provided. If you have to cancel for any reason, we would credit your account in the discounted amount of the visit for the cancellation.

Pricing

  • 15-20 Minute Potty Break Package - $260
  • 30 Minute Package - $300
  • 45 Minute Package - $380
  • 60 Minute Package - $460

Additional Fees

Last Minute Requests

  • If a service is requested less than 24 hours in advance, there will be an additional $5 surcharge.

Early and Late Walks/Visits

  • Normal visit hours are from 8 am until 8 pm. We will provide visits outside of our normal business hours for an additional $5 per walk/visit.

Cancellations

  • During non-holiday periods, cancellations must be received no later than 24 hours in advance. Less than 24-hour notice will result in client being billed 50% of the cancelled service. Cancellation fees may be waived due to inclement weather conditions. Cancellations made the day of service, vacation visits, and during holiday weeks and peak vacation periods may not be refunded or credited.

Holiday Services

  • Additional $10 charge for each visit on the following holidays:
  • New Years Day, Easter, Memorial Day, Fourth of July, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, New Years Eve
