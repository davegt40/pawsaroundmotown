About Us

Who We Are

Paws Around Motown is owned by me, Becky Lea, and was founded for one reason, I love animals! Paws Around Motown is a bonded and insured professional dog walking and pet sitting company serving Berkley, Beverly Hills, Birmingham, Bloomfield Hills and Twp, Center Line, Clawson, Commerce Twp, Ferndale, Franklin, Hazel Park, Huntington Woods, Lathrup Village, Madison Heights, Oak Park, Pleasant Ridge, Roseville, Royal Oak, Southfield, Troy, Walled Lake, Warren, and West Bloomfield.

Our Dogs

I have been around dogs my entire life and currently have two of my own, a 5 lb. Yorkie Poo named Winston whom I got as a puppy, and a 10 lb. Toy Poodle named Mocha whom I rescued two years ago.

Why We Do It

After working in retail management for more than half of my life and completely burning out, I decided to become a nanny, because I love caring for others. In thinking over the past few years about what my passions are and what I truly wanted to do with the rest of my life, I realized my true passion is caring for animals! I began volunteering at a local animal rescue organization walking dogs during their adoption events and thought, “How can I do this all the time?”. The next step was a logical one for me, I created Paws Around Motown and now I spend my days filled with joy and excitement.
